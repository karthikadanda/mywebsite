# Quick Deploy - XPRINT Solutions Website

## Fastest Way to Get Online (5 Minutes)

### Step 1: Go to GitHub
1. Open https://github.com
2. Click "Sign up" (if you don't have account)
3. Create free account

### Step 2: Create Repository
1. Click "New repository" (green button)
2. Repository name: `xprint-solutions`
3. Description: `XPRINT Solutions - Ameerpet Printing Business`
4. Select **Public**
5. Click "Create repository"

### Step 3: Upload Your Files
1. Click "Add file" > "Upload files"
2. Upload these files:
   - `index.html`
   - `script.js`
   - `README.md`
   - `sw.js`
   - `robots.txt`
   - `sitemap.xml`
   - `.gitignore`
3. Scroll down, type "Initial commit"
4. Click "Commit changes"

### Step 4: Enable GitHub Pages
1. Click "Settings" tab
2. Scroll down to "Pages" section
3. Under "Build and deployment", click "Edit"
4. Source: "Deploy from a branch"
5. Branch: "main" > folder: "/ (root)"
6. Click "Save"

### Step 5: Wait & Get URL
- Wait 2-5 minutes
- Your site will be live at: `https://[your-username].github.io/xprint-solutions/`
